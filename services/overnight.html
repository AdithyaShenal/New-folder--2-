What is OVERNIGHT DELIVERING

Overnight delivery is a shipping service that guarantees delivery of a package or item within one business day, typically within 24 hours, from the time it's picked up to the time it's delivered to the recipient. This service is offered by various courier companies and is often used for time-sensitive or urgent shipments when regular shipping methods would take longer. Overnight delivery often comes with additional costs due to the speed and priority of the service. It's widely used in business, e-commerce, and personal shipments when fast delivery is crucial.
